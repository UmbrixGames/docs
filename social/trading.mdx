## Direct Trading

Trade directly with another player for instant exchanges.

### Trade Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/trade <player>` | Send trade request |
| `/trade accept` | Accept incoming request |
| `/trade deny` | Decline trade request |

### How to Trade

<Steps>
  <Step title="Send Request">
    Use `/trade <playername>` to request a trade
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Items">
    Click items in your inventory to add them
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Currency">
    Specify gold amount if needed
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Both players must click "Confirm" to complete
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Always verify the items before confirming! Trades cannot be reversed.
</Warning>

## Auction House

The Auction House is the central marketplace for all players.

### Auction Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/ah` | Open Auction House |
| `/ah sell <price>` | List held item for sale |
| `/ah search <term>` | Search for items |
| `/ah expired` | View expired listings |
| `/ah collect` | Collect sold item profits |

### Listing Items

<Steps>
  <Step title="Hold the Item">
    Put the item you want to sell in your hand
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Price">
    Use `/ah sell <price>` to list it
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait">
    Your item is now visible to all players
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect">
    Use `/ah collect` to get your gold after it sells
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Auction Fees

| Item Tier | Listing Fee | Sold Commission |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| Common | 1 gold | 5% |
| Uncommon | 5 gold | 5% |
| Rare | 10 gold | 5% |
| Epic | 25 gold | 7% |
| Legendary | 50 gold | 10% |

<Tip>
  Price your items competitively! Check what others are selling for with `/ah search`.
</Tip>

## Player Shops

Create a permanent shop on your island for passive income.

### Shop Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/shop create` | Create a shop sign |
| `/shop stock` | Add items to your shop |
| `/shop prices` | View/set prices |
| `/shop collect` | Collect earnings |

### Setting Up a Shop

1. Place a sign where you want the shop
2. Use `/shop create` while looking at the sign
3. Stock items and set prices
4. Players can buy when you're offline!

## Currency

### Gold

Gold is the primary currency in Tenebris:

- Earned from quests, dungeons, and selling items
- Used for trading, repairs, and services
- View balance with `/balance` or `/bal`

### Transferring Gold

Send gold to another player:

```bash
/pay <player> <amount>
```

<Note>
  There is no fee for direct gold transfers between players.
</Note>

## Trade Safety

Tenebris includes several safety features:

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Trade Lock** | Both must confirm before completion |
| **Scam Protection** | Can't modify after the other confirms |
| **Trade Log** | All trades are logged for support |
| **Item Verification** | Items display full stats in trade window |

### Reporting Scams

If you believe you've been scammed:

1. Do not trade further with the player
2. Take screenshots of your trade history
3. Open a ticket on Discord
4. Staff will investigate and take action

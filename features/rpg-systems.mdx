## Experience & Leveling

Earn experience through various activities:

- **Combat:** Defeat mobs and dungeon bosses
- **Quests:** Complete story and side quests
- **Dungeons:** Clear dungeon challenges
- **Crafting:** Forge items and upgrade gear

### Level Benefits

Each level provides:
- Increased base stats (health, damage, defense)
- Skill points to unlock abilities
- Access to higher-tier equipment
- New content unlocks

## Skills System

Skills are divided into categories based on your activities:

### Combat Skills

| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Swordsmanship** | Increases melee damage and combo attacks |
| **Archery** | Improves arrow damage and accuracy |
| **Magic** | Enhances spell power and mana regeneration |
| **Defense** | Reduces incoming damage |

### Gathering Skills

| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Mining** | Better ore drops, faster mining |
| **Farming** | Increased crop yields |
| **Fishing** | Rare catches, treasure finds |
| **Foraging** | Better wood and plant drops |

### Crafting Skills

| Skill | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Forging** | Craft higher tier equipment |
| **Enchanting** | Apply custom enchantments |
| **Alchemy** | Create powerful potions |

<Tip>
  Focus on skills that complement your chosen class for maximum effectiveness!
</Tip>

## Custom Enchantments

Tenebris features unique enchantments beyond vanilla Minecraft:

### Weapon Enchantments

- **Soul Steal:** Heal on hit
- **Void Strike:** Ignore armor
- **Thunder:** Chain lightning damage
- **Blaze:** Set enemies on fire with bonus damage

### Armor Enchantments

- **Fortitude:** Increased max health
- **Evasion:** Chance to dodge attacks
- **Reflection:** Return damage to attackers
- **Regeneration:** Passive health recovery

### Tool Enchantments

- **Vein Miner:** Mine entire ore veins
- **Auto-Smelt:** Automatically smelt ores
- **Magnetic:** Auto-pickup nearby items

## Stats & Attributes

Your character has core stats that affect combat:

| Stat | Effect |
|------|--------|
| **Strength** | Physical damage |
| **Intelligence** | Magic damage and mana pool |
| **Dexterity** | Attack speed and critical chance |
| **Vitality** | Health and defense |
| **Luck** | Drop rates and critical damage |

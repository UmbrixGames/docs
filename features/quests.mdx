Tenebris features a rich narrative experience with main story quests, side quests, and dynamic cutscenes that bring the world to life.

## Story Overview

You arrive in the world of Tenebris as darkness threatens to consume everything. Through your journey, you'll uncover ancient secrets, forge alliances, and ultimately decide the fate of the realm.

<Note>
  The main story unfolds as you progress through the game. New chapters unlock as you reach higher levels and complete previous quests.
</Note>

## Quest Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/quest` | Open quest journal |
| `/quest active` | View current quests |
| `/quest track <id>` | Track a specific quest |
| `/quest abandon <id>` | Abandon a quest |

## Main Story Quests

Main story quests are marked with a special icon and provide:

- **Cutscenes:** Fully animated story moments
- **Major rewards:** Unique equipment and large XP bonuses
- **World unlocks:** Access to new areas and content
- **Character development:** Meet key NPCs and factions

### Story Progression

<Steps>
  <Step title="Prologue: Awakening">
    Learn the basics and discover your purpose (Level 1-5)

  <Step title="Chapter 1: The Gathering Storm">

## Side Quests

Side quests expand the world and offer additional rewards:

### Types of Side Quests

| Type | Description | Reward |
|------|-------------|--------|
| **Exploration** | Discover hidden locations | XP, materials |
| **Collection** | Gather specific items | Currency, equipment |
| **Combat** | Defeat certain enemies | XP, dungeon tokens |
| **Crafting** | Create items for NPCs | Recipes, materials |
| **Social** | Help other players | Unique titles |

### Finding Side Quests

- Look for NPCs with quest markers (‚ùó)
- Explore new areas for hidden questgivers
- Check guild boards for community requests
- Some quests trigger from item discoveries

## Daily & Weekly Quests

Repeatable quests that refresh regularly:

### Daily Quests

<Info>
  Reset every day at midnight (server time)
</Info>

- Complete 3 dungeon runs
- Defeat 50 enemies
- Gather 100 resources
- Help 1 player

### Weekly Quests

<Info>
  Reset every Monday at midnight (server time)
</Info>

- Complete all daily quests 5 times
- Clear a Tier 3+ dungeon
- Upgrade an item
- Participate in a guild event

## Cutscenes

Tenebris features cinematic cutscenes at key story moments:

- **Triggered automatically** at quest milestones
- **Skippable** if you've seen them before
- **Voice lines** and dramatic camera work
- **Player choices** affect outcomes in some scenes

<Tip>
  Pay attention to dialogue! Some choices affect quest outcomes and NPC relationships.
</Tip>

## Quest Rewards

### Reward Types

| Reward | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Experience** | Level up your character |
| **Gold** | Server currency for trading |
| **Equipment** | Weapons, armor, accessories |
| **Materials** | Crafting components |
| **Titles** | Display under your name |
| **Cosmetics** | Unique appearances |

### Bonus Rewards

Some quests offer bonus rewards for:

- Completing within a time limit
- Finishing without dying
- Finding all hidden objectives
- Making specific dialogue choices

Tenebris combines classic Skyblock gameplay with enhanced progression systems that tie into the RPG elements of the server.

## Your Island

Your island is your home base in Tenebris. Unlike traditional Skyblock, your island connects to the greater world and serves as your hub for crafting, storage, and preparation.

### Island Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/island create` | Create your personal island |
| `/island home` | Teleport to your island |
| `/island settings` | Configure island permissions |
| `/island invite <player>` | Invite a player to your island |
| `/island kick <player>` | Remove a player from your island |
| `/island level` | Check your island's level |

## Progression System

Your island progresses through levels based on the blocks and structures you build.

### Island Levels

<Steps>
  <Step title="Starter Island (Level 1-10)">

  </Step>
  <Step title="Established (Level 11-25)">
    Mob spawners, advanced farms, basic forging access
  </Step>
  <Step title="Advanced (Level 26-50)">
    Dungeon access, guild features, trading unlocked
  </Step>
  <Step title="Master (Level 51+)">
    End-game content, elite dungeons, legendary forging
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resource Generation

### Cobblestone Generator

Every island starts with a basic cobblestone generator. As you progress, you can upgrade it to generate more valuable ores:

- **Tier 1:** Cobblestone only
- **Tier 2:** Coal, Iron
- **Tier 3:** Gold, Lapis, Redstone
- **Tier 4:** Diamond, Emerald
- **Tier 5:** Custom Tenebris ores

### Farming

Grow crops to earn resources and materials for quests:

- Standard Minecraft crops
- Custom Tenebris plants with unique properties
- Magical ingredients for forging

## Island Upgrades

Invest resources to unlock permanent island upgrades:

| Upgrade | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| **Island Size** | Expand your building area |
| **Generator Tier** | Better ore generation |
| **Mob Spawner Slots** | Place more spawners |
| **Visitor Capacity** | Allow more guests |

## Class Overview

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Warrior">
    ## Warrior
    
    The Warrior excels in close combat, wielding heavy weapons and armor to dominate the battlefield.
    
    ### Role
    - **Primary:** Melee DPS / Tank
    - **Difficulty:** Beginner-friendly
    - **Playstyle:** Get up close and deal devastating damage
    
    ### Strengths
    - Highest health pool and defense
    - Powerful melee attacks with combo potential
    - Can take hits that would fell other classes
    - Ideal for solo play and tanking dungeons
    
    ### Weaknesses
    - Limited ranged options
    - Slower movement speed in heavy armor
    - Mana-intensive for active abilities
    
    ### Key Abilities
    | Ability | Description |
    |---------|-------------|
    | **Berserker Rage** | Increase damage but take more damage |
    | **Shield Wall** | Block all incoming damage briefly |
    | **Cleave** | Hit multiple enemies in an arc |
    | **War Cry** | Buff nearby allies' attack speed |
    
    ### Recommended For
    - New players learning the game
    - Those who enjoy tanking in dungeons
    - Solo players who want survivability
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Mage">
    ## Mage
    
    The Mage harnesses arcane power to devastate enemies from afar and support allies.
    
    ### Role
    - **Primary:** Ranged DPS / Support
    - **Difficulty:** Intermediate
    - **Playstyle:** Cast powerful spells from a safe distance
    
    ### Strengths
    - Highest damage potential with spells
    - Excellent crowd control abilities
    - Can support allies with buffs and heals
    - Area-of-effect damage for groups
    
    ### Weaknesses
    - Lowest health and defense
    - Relies on mana management
    - Vulnerable if enemies get close
    
    ### Key Abilities
    | Ability | Description |
    |---------|-------------|
    | **Fireball** | Launch an explosive projectile |
    | **Ice Nova** | Freeze nearby enemies |
    | **Arcane Shield** | Create a protective barrier |
    | **Healing Light** | Restore health to allies |
    
    ### Recommended For
    - Players who enjoy ranged combat
    - Those who like strategic gameplay
    - Support-oriented players
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Archer">
    ## Archer
    
    The Archer strikes from the shadows with precision and deadly critical hits.
    
    ### Role
    - **Primary:** Ranged DPS
    - **Difficulty:** Advanced
    - **Playstyle:** High mobility, precision strikes
    
    ### Strengths
    - Highest critical hit damage
    - Excellent mobility and evasion
    - Strong single-target damage
    - Effective at kiting enemies
    
    ### Weaknesses
    - Requires good aim and positioning
    - Struggles against large groups
    - Medium survivability
    
    ### Key Abilities
    | Ability | Description |
    |---------|-------------|
    | **Power Shot** | Charged arrow with bonus damage |
    | **Rain of Arrows** | AoE damage in a target area |
    | **Dodge Roll** | Quick evasive maneuver |
    | **Mark Target** | Increase damage against one enemy |
    
    ### Recommended For
    - Skilled players who enjoy precision
    - Those who like mobile playstyles
    - Players focused on DPS
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Choosing Your Class

<Note>
  You can change your class later, but it requires a special quest and resets your class-specific progress. Choose carefully!
</Note>

### Class Selection Command

Use this command to select or view your class:

```bash
/class select <warrior|mage|archer>
/class info
/class stats
```

## Class Synergy in Parties

When forming a dungeon party, consider class balance:

| Party Role | Recommended Class |
|------------|-------------------|
| **Tank** | Warrior |
| **Healer** | Mage |
| **DPS** | Archer or Warrior |
| **Support** | Mage |

<Tip>
  A balanced party with all three classes can tackle the hardest dungeons more easily!
</Tip>

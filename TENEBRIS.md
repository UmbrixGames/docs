# Tenebris

**Tenebris** is the *premium Minecraft server experience* developed by **UmbrixGames**, positioned as a next-generation Skyblock world with RPG and adventure features. 

## Overview

**Tenebris** is a custom **Minecraft server** hosted by UmbrixGames that combines traditional Skyblock progression with elements of RPG systems, dungeons, and expansive world exploration. 

As described on the official site:

> *“The next evolution of Skyblock. Dungeons, RPG systems, and a vast world waiting to be conquered. Join the elite.”* 

The server is positioned as **online soon** and branded as a *premium Minecraft experience*. 

## Features (Based on Official Positioning)

Based on the *Tenebris* landing page from UmbrixGames, key conceptual areas include:

### Skyblock Core

- A customized Skyblock foundation with progression beyond vanilla mechanics. 

### RPG Integration

- Systems that extend survival gameplay with role-playing elements, quests, or character progression. 

### Dungeons

- Dedicated dungeon content for cooperative and solo exploration challenges. 

### Expansive World

- A broader Minecraft world experience beyond traditional Skyblock islands.

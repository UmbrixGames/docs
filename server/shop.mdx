## Empyrian Crystals

**Empyrian Crystals** are the premium currency in Tenebris, used to purchase exclusive items, cosmetics, and bonuses.

### How to Top-Up

You can purchase Empyrian Crystals through the official UmbrixGames Top-Up platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Link Your Account">
    Ensure your UmbrixGames account is linked from within the game
  </Step>
  <Step title="Visit the Top-Up Page">
    Go to the official UmbrixGames shop
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Package">
    Choose the Empyrian Crystals package you want
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Follow the payment process to complete your purchase
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Your Crystals">
    Log in to the game to receive your purchase
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Initial Top-Up Bonus

First-time buyers may receive bonus crystals! Check the status of your Initial Top-Up Bonus by visiting:

**Shop → Empyrian Crystals Top-Up** in the game interface.

<Note>
  The Initial Top-Up Bonus is only available once per account.
</Note>

## Important Top-Up Information

<Warning>
  Please read these important points before making a purchase.
</Warning>

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|

After a successful payment:

1. Your purchase is applied to your account automatically

3. If you don't receive your purchase after **30 minutes**, contact support

## Blessing of the Thousands Sun

The **Blessing of the Thousands Sun** is a premium subscription that provides daily rewards over 30 days.

### What You Get

| Benefit | Amount |
|---------|--------|
| **Immediate Bonus** | 300 Empyrian Crystals (instant) |
| **Daily Login Reward** | 90 Primogems per day |
| **Duration** | 30 days |

<Check>
  **Total Value:** 300 Empyrian Crystals + 2,700 Primogems (90 × 30 days)
</Check>

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase the Blessing">
    Buy the Blessing of the Thousands Sun from the shop
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Instant Bonus">
    Get 300 Empyrian Crystals immediately
  </Step>
  <Step title="Claim Daily Rewards">
    Log in each day to claim 90 Primogems
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enjoy for 30 Days">
    The blessing lasts for 30 days from purchase
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Important Rules

<Warning>
  Read these rules carefully before purchasing.
</Warning>

| Rule | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Maximum Duration** | You can only extend the Blessing if remaining duration is **180 days or less** |
| **Purchase Limit** | Cannot purchase if remaining duration exceeds 180 days |
| **No Refunds** | Primogems not claimed via daily login are **not refunded** |

<Tip>
  Set a daily reminder to log in and claim your Primogems! Unclaimed rewards are lost.
</Tip>

### Extending Your Blessing

You can stack multiple Blessing purchases to extend the duration:

- Each purchase adds **30 days** to your remaining time
- Maximum stackable duration: **180 days**
- Cannot purchase beyond the 180-day limit

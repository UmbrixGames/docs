By playing on Tenebris, you agree to follow these rules. Violations may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans depending on severity.

## General Rules

<Warning>
  Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse. Please read them carefully.
</Warning>

### 1. Respect All Players

- Treat everyone with respect and courtesy
- No harassment, bullying, or discrimination
- No hate speech or slurs of any kind
- Avoid excessive toxicity or negativity

### 2. No Cheating or Exploits

Prohibited modifications include:

| Forbidden | Allowed |
|-----------|---------|
| X-ray / Cave finder | Optifine |
| Kill aura / Aimbot | Minimaps (without cave mode) |
| Speed / Fly hacks | Inventory sorting |
| Auto-clickers | Shaders |
| Macros that give advantage | Armor/status HUDs |

<Note>
  If you're unsure whether a mod is allowed, ask staff before using it.
</Note>

### 3. No Scamming

- Honor all trade agreements
- No misleading pricing or fake items
- Real-money trading (RMT) is strictly forbidden
- Report scammers to staff immediately

### 4. No Inappropriate Content

- Keep chat family-friendly
- No NSFW content in any form
- No advertising other servers
- No spamming or excessive caps

### 5. English in Global Chat

- Use English in global channels
- Other languages are allowed in party/guild chat
- Use `/msg` for private conversations

### Chat Commands

```bash
/ignore <player>   # Block messages from a player
/report <player>   # Report a rule breaker
```

## Gameplay Rules

### Island Building

- No offensive builds or symbols
- Don't intentionally lag the server with redstone
- Respect other players' islands

### PvP Rules

- PvP is only enabled in designated zones
- No combat logging (disconnecting during fights)
- No spawn camping or griefing

### Economy

- No exploiting bugs for currency
- Report economy exploits immediately
- Excessive wealth hoarding may be investigated

## Guild/Faction Rules

- Guild names must be appropriate
- No impersonating staff or other guilds
- Guild wars must follow the war system
- No harassment under the guise of "roleplay"

## Punishments

### Punishment Tiers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Warning">
    First minor offense - verbal or written warning
  </Step>
  <Step title="Temporary Mute">
    Chat rule violations - 30 min to 24 hours
  </Step>
  <Step title="Temporary Ban">
    Serious violations - 1 day to 30 days
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permanent Ban">
    Severe or repeated violations
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Instant Bans

These result in immediate permanent bans:

- Hacking/cheating
- DDoS threats
- Doxing or real-life threats
- Extreme hate speech
- Chargebacks on purchases

## Appeals

If you believe you were punished unfairly:

<Card title="Open an Appeal" icon="scale-balanced" href="https://discord.gg/umbrixgames">
  Create a ticket in our Discord to appeal your punishment
</Card>

### Appeal Requirements

- Be respectful and patient
- Provide your username and punishment reason
- Explain your side honestly
- Wait for staff review (usually 24-72 hours)

## Reporting Players

### How to Report

1. **In-game:** Use `/report <player> <reason>`
2. **Discord:** Open a ticket with evidence
3. **Include:** Screenshots, video, timestamps

<Tip>
  Reports with evidence are handled much faster!
</Tip>

## Staff Team

Our staff are here to help. Look for these ranks:

| Rank | Role |
|------|------|
| **Helper** | Answers questions, basic moderation |
| **Moderator** | Full moderation powers |
| **Admin** | Server management |
| **Developer** | Technical issues only |

<Note>
  Staff will **never** ask for your password or personal information.
</Note>
